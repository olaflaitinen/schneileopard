## Attribution

Schnéileopard builds upon the Scala ecosystem and draws inspiration from scientific computing libraries in Python, R, and other languages. We are grateful to all projects, researchers, and institutions that have contributed to the broader context of computational biology and systems medicine.

## Founder Statement

Schnéileopard is created as part of PhD research in Systems and Molecular Biomedicine. The goal is to provide the research community with a reliable, maintainable, type-safe toolkit that bridges the gap between molecular biology data and computational analysis. This project embodies principles of open science, academic rigor, and software engineering excellence.
